# Analysis of Indian Engineering Companies,  Research Dashboard
**Software Used:** Power BI | **Domain:** Indian Listed Engineering Companies  
**Research Universe:** 7 Companies + 35-Company Median Benchmark

, -

## Project Overview
Goal: Interactive research dashboard to compare and evaluate listed Indian engineering companies across valuation, profitability, returns, quarterly performance metrics.

Enables buy/hold/avoid decisions by surfacing financial "signal", all-in-one-view,  mimics a standard equity research workflow.

## Dashboard Metrics & What They Answer


### 1. ROE, Pie Chart
Compares ROE distribution across Last Year, 3Y, 5Y, and 10Y.  
**Question answered:** Is the company generating consistent returns for shareholders over time, or is recent ROE inflated?

### 2. Key Financial Metrics, Line Chart
Compares Total Assets, Total Liabilities, Cash Conversion Cycle, Operating Profit, Profit Before Tax, Net Profit, Net Cash Flow.  
**Question answered:** What does the overall financial structure and operational efficiency of the selected company look like?


### 3. ROCE, P/E & Quarterly Variance, Bar Chart
Side-by-side comparison of ROCE %, P/E, Qtr Profit variance, & Qtr Sales variance across companies.  
**Question answered:** Which companies are generating the best returns on capital relative to their valuation?

### 4. Return on Equity, Multi-Year Bar Chart
Compares ROE across 4 time horizons (Last Year / 3Y / 5Y / 10Y).  
**Question answered:** Is ROE improving or deteriorating over time?


### 5. Valuation & Market Overview, Donut Chart
Breakdown of Market Cap distribution across the coverage universe.  
**Question answered:** How does the selected company's size compare within this peer group?

### 6. P/E Gauge
Visual gauge showing current P/E against the range (0 to ~120).  
**Question answered:** Is the stock cheap or expensive relative to its earnings?


### 7. Financial Summary Table
Snapshot of key metrics: Cash Conversion Cycle, Net Cash Flow, Net Profit, Operating Profit, Total Assets, Total Liabilities.  
**Question answered:** Quick financial health check at a glance.

### 8. Sales Qtr KPI Card
Current quarter revenue in Rs. Cr. for the selected company.  
**Question answered:** What is the most recent top line performance?

## Key Metrics Glossary
| Metric | What It Measures |
|, -|, -|
| **ROCE %** | Efficiency of capital deployment |
| **P/E** | Price paid per rupee of earnings (valuation) |
| **Div Yld %** | Income return to shareholders |
| **NP Qtr** | Latest quarterly net profit (Rs. Cr.) |
| **Qtr Profit Var %** | YoY change in quarterly profit |
| **Qtr Sales Var %** | YoY change in quarterly revenue |
| **Cash Conversion Cycle** | Days taken to convert operations into cash |
| **ROE (Multi-year)** | Shareholder return efficiencey over time |

-

## Selected Key Insights
- **Esab India** leads the peer group in ROCE at 70% (vs the 35-company median of 19.33%)
- **PTC Industries** trades at the highest P/E (359x) despite registering only 7.75% ROCE
- **KRN Heat Exchange** is trading at a steep 96.27 P/E with only 20.71% ROCE
- **Kirloskar Industries** is available at the cheaper P/E (30x) with steady ROCE
- **HBL Engineering** has reported a -53% shock in profit in the latest quarter despite a solid 26% ROCE.

## How to Use This Dashboard
1. Download `dashboard.pbix`
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop** (free from Microsoft)
3. Use the **Name slicer** (top right) to select any company
4. All visuals update automatically to reflect the selected name
